⚖️ Compare & Contrast

Make better decisions
with side-by-side clarity

Prompts for comparing ideas, products, arguments, and options so you can see exactly what matters.

01
Compare [Option A] and [Option B] side by side. For each, cover: core purpose, key strengths, main weaknesses, best use case, and who it's ideal for. End with a clear recommendation based on [my specific situation or priority]. Be direct — don't hedge.
02
I'm trying to decide between [Option A] and [Option B]. My priorities are: [list 3-5 things that matter most to you]. Score each option on each priority from 1-10, explain the scores briefly, then give me a weighted recommendation based on what I said matters most.
03
Create a detailed comparison table for [Topic — e.g. 3 project management tools, 4 programming languages, 5 marketing strategies]. Rows should be the key evaluation criteria, columns should be each option. After the table, write 2-3 sentences on which option stands out and why.
04
Compare the arguments for and against [position or decision]. Give me the 4 strongest arguments FOR, then the 4 strongest arguments AGAINST. Be steelman on both sides — present each as powerfully as possible. Then tell me which side has the stronger overall case and why.
05
I'm comparing [Thing A] from [time period or context A] with [Thing B] from [time period or context B]. What are the meaningful similarities, the key differences, and what does comparing them reveal that looking at each one alone wouldn't show? Give me genuine insight, not just surface-level observations.
06
Compare how [different groups, experts, or schools of thought] approach [topic or problem]. What does each approach prioritize? Where do they fundamentally disagree, and where do they actually agree more than they'd admit? Which approach do you think has the most evidence behind it and why?
07
I need to compare [X number] of vendors / tools / candidates for [purpose]. Here is my list: [paste list]. Evaluate each on: [your criteria]. Flag any dealbreakers, highlight the top 2, and give me a final recommendation with your reasoning. Be concise and decisive.
08
Here are two pieces of writing / two strategies / two proposals on the same topic. Compare them critically: which is better argued, which has stronger evidence, which has more logical gaps, and which would be more persuasive to [target audience]? Here are the two pieces: [paste both]
