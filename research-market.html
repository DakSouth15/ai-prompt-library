Know your market
before you move

Prompts for competitive analysis, industry trends, customer insights, and strategic market overviews.

01
Give me a market overview of the [industry] industry. Cover: total market size and growth rate, key players and their approximate market share, the main customer segments, the most important trends driving the market right now, and the biggest threats or disruptions on the horizon.
02
Do a competitive analysis of [Company A], [Company B], and [Company C]. For each, cover: their positioning and target customer, pricing strategy, key differentiators, apparent strengths, and visible weaknesses. Then tell me where the gaps are that a new entrant could exploit.
03
I'm launching [product/service] in [market]. Help me identify my target customer in detail: demographics, psychographics, pain points, how they currently solve this problem, what they'd pay, and where to reach them. Then suggest which segment to prioritize first and why.
04
Analyze the competitive landscape for [product category]. Who are the incumbents, who are the challengers, who are the niche players? What are customers most commonly complaining about with existing solutions? What would make someone switch from their current provider?
05
What are the most important macro trends affecting the [industry] industry over the next 3-5 years? Consider: technology shifts, regulatory changes, demographic trends, economic factors, and behavioral changes in customers. Which trend is most likely to create the biggest disruption?
06
Help me do a SWOT analysis for [company or business idea]. Be specific and honest â€” don't just list generic strengths. For weaknesses and threats, be direct about what could actually kill this. For opportunities, focus on the ones that are realistic given the strengths.
07
I want to understand the customer journey for [product/service category]. Map out each stage from awareness to purchase to retention: what triggers the need, how customers research options, what drives the decision, what causes churn, and what creates loyalty. Where are the biggest friction points?
08
What are the pricing models used in the [industry] market? Compare the approaches: what price points exist, how do different players justify their pricing, what does the research say about price sensitivity in this category, and what pricing strategy would you recommend for a new entrant trying to gain market share quickly?
